Quiche

  • READY IN: 1h
  • SERVES: 2-4
  • YIELD: 1 quiche
  • UNITS: US

Ingredients

5 large eggs

3⁄4 cup milk

1 frozen 9-inch deep dish pie shell

2 cups shredded cheese or 2 cups chopped drained cooked vegetables or 2 cups diced deli meat, any type (or chop up leftover slices) or 2 cups any other cooked diced meat or 2 cups ground beef or 2 cups seafood or 2 cups poultry

Directions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit.

Beat eggs in a large mixing bowl.

Add filling and milk and mix well.

Pour mixture into frozen pie crust.

Place quiche on cookie sheet and bake on the middle oven rack for 45-50 minutes, or until top is browned and a knife inserted in center comes out clean.

Serve with a crisp green salad or maybe some fresh fruit on the side.

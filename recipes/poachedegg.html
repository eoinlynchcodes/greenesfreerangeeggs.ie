Poached Egg

  • READY IN: 3 minutes

Ingredients

Eggs

Water

Directions

Fill saucepan with about 3 inches (8 cm) of water. Heat until the water simmers gently. Break a cold egg into small dish or saucer. Holding dish just above simmering water, gently slip the egg into the water. Cook in barely simmering water until white is set and yolk is cooked as desired for between 3 to 5 minutes. Remove the eggs with slotted spoon, drain well and enjoy!
